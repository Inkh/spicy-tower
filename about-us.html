About us!

Team Story

We are a diverse multifunctional team, with a huge passion for coding. We all have very diverse backgrounds ranging from military commander, designer to teacher and legal. Our diverse backgrounds and experiences bring mangy advantages to our team. We are huge believers in never-ending knowledge and are on a conquest to keep learning. We're excited to make cutting edge break throughs in technology and hope to share our knowledge with the world.

Our goal is to implement a jumping platform game that would bring back a sense of of nostalgia. We were inspired by icy tower and other examples online. In order to tackle some of the challenges with game physics we utilized phaser 3.11. Although, we initially struggled finding documentation, with a lot of research and experimentatoin we were able to complete our Spicy Tower version 1.0.0.
